 My First Social Engineering Exercise

Exercise 1: Phishing Email Analysis

 Objective
Analyze a phishing email to identify social engineering tactics.

 Tools Used
- Email header analyzer
- URL scanner
- VirusTotal

Steps Performed
1. Email Examination: Checked sender address, subject line, and content
2. Link Analysis: Examined embedded URLs for malicious intent
3. Attachment Check: Analyzed any attachments safely
4. Social Engineering Tactics: Identified persuasion techniques

 Findings
- Urgency: Email created false urgency ("Your account will be deleted!")
- Authority: Pretended to be from legitimate organization
- Familiarity: Used personal information found online

 Prevention Tips
- Never click links in unexpected emails
- Verify sender addresses
- Use multi-factor authentication
